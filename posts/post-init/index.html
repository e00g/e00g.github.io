a very warm welcome to you all!

after working years as a software developer in the industry, I figured out that I’m not happy with what I’m doing. I had started learning programming by digging the things which was looking a black-box to me, which had something waiting to be discovered. and then the wind of the life made my sail to go and I became a programmer, worked with several programming languages for companies which are doing some cool(!) stuff in different sectors.

looking back has helped me realizing why I want to work as a programmer, what I want to do and what I don’t want to do.

and yes, that river finally fell into the sea: we will do here what we love. I’m planning to create a your-quick-library to learn several low-level topics like system programming, kernel development, arm assembly etc. we will learn these lovely things all together.

I’m not sure if I can change my career someday as I do lovely things in the job, but at least, having a nicely deep knowledge about where you deploy your application will always be helpful.

that’s enough talk (:
